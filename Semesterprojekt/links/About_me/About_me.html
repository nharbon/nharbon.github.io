ABOUT ME

My Name is Noah Harbon. I was born on the 23.12.2004 in Ormskirk, England.

I lived in Southport, England for three years till I moved to Austria. We moved to Gallneukirchen were we lived at a house with our Grandparents.

After five years we moved to Enns, Austria were I have been living for eight years now. At the time we moved in I was in the last year of kindergarden. After the kindergarden I went to Enns primary school.

In 2016 I started going to the Enns Gymnasium. I finished the school in 2019. After the Gymnasium I started at the HTL Leonding, where I am now.
